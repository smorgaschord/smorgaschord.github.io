Callback Audition Songs

Congratulations! To prepare for the callback audition, we'd like you to learn excerpts from two songs, below. (In the mp3s we provide below, you can use your computer's left-right balance to hear all or none of your part.) If the recording doesn't match the sheet music on some note, feel free to sing what you think sounds best. During the audition, we will sing these songs together. Using a printed copy to sing with us is fine!

1. Partial song 1

"You're the Inspiration" (you sing harmony):
  • Your part is the "Soprano" second line from the top (marked "This line")
  • Sing from the beginning through measure 39 (top of page 9)
  • See the sheet music [PDF]
  • Practice with us singing it [MP3] with your part on the left channel

2. Partial song 2

"Good Vibrations" (you sing melody/lead, and theremin in the chorus):
  • Your part is the "Soprano" lead (melody and theramin, marked "Soprano" throughout)
  • Sing from the beginning through measure 32 (top of page 6)
  • See the sheet music [PDF] or sheet music as individual pages [JPEG within a zip file]
  • Practice with us singing it in rehearsal [MP3]
  • For reference only: An slightly different older arrangement (published on our web site) of us singing it [MP3]

3. Review Smorgaschord group agreements

To make clear our shared goals and commitments, we've put together two agreements that we each sign upon joining Smorgaschord. Please look them over and let us know if you have questions or suggestions.
  • Goals and Conflict Agreement
  • Property Agreement

Optional/Extra Credit

If you like, feel free to do one of the following, but these are totally optional.

  • memorize any audition songs,
  • sing any of the audition songs with us to the end,
  • show us one of your arrangements, or
  • showcase something else you do well
